# opentrawl-demo — session prompts

Paste one of these into an opus session in ~/code/opentrawl.

## Bootstrap (first session — builds the pipeline, makes demo #1)

> Load the opentrawl-demo skill and follow it. The demo/ scaffold
> doesn't exist yet: build it (Remotion player, transcript schema,
> make entrypoint, nix-pinned devenv per the skill, git-ignored
> demo/raw/ and demo/out/), interviewing me on the frozen aesthetics
> before you commit them. Then demo #1: propose 3 candidate killer
> queries — cross-source joins first per the skill's wow hierarchy —
> with the one-sentence claim and narrative shape for each. I pick,
> you take it through the full pipeline. Stop at every Josh gate the
> skill defines.

## Steady-state (any future video)

> Load the opentrawl-demo skill. Make a demo of: <query or feature>.
> State the claim and shape first; scaffold exists — record →
> sanitize gate → render both formats → show me.

## Refresh (product output changed)

> Load the opentrawl-demo skill. Re-record every transcript in
> demo/transcripts/ against current binaries, show me sanitization
> diffs only where output actually changed, re-render all, flag any
> video whose claim the product no longer proves.
